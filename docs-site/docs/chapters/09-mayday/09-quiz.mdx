<QuizQuestion
  question="What is the 'Open Cockpit' anti-pattern?"
  options={[
    "Using AI tools in a public space where others can see your screen",
    "Pasting production credentials, API keys, or proprietary code into AI prompts — exposing secrets to external servers",
    "Using open-source AI tools instead of commercial ones",
    "Leaving your IDE open with AI suggestions visible during screen sharing"
  ]}
  correctIndex={1}
  explanation="The Open Cockpit anti-pattern is pasting secrets into AI prompts. Like an open cockpit at 30,000 feet, everything inside is exposed. Production API keys, database connection strings, OAuth tokens — once pasted, they exist on external servers. Even if the provider does not train on your data, the data was transmitted and may appear in logs. The fix: replace secrets with placeholders before pasting."
/>
